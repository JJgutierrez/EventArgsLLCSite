Is EventArgs LLC a Good Fit?

We work best with companies that match specific architectural and business profiles.

  • You use Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365, and SharePoint extensively.
  • Your documentation is scattered, but readable and text-based (no paper scanning required).
  • Your developers spend significant time reviewing repetitive PR cycles or release tasks.
  • You require strict data privacy, isolated model hosting, and verifiable output auditing.

Bad Fit

  • You need high-level "AI strategy slides" without any codebase execution.
  • You want to build public customer-facing support bots without human guardrails or safety nets.
  • Your documentation is unstructured image scans, un-OCR'd files, or handwritten logs.
